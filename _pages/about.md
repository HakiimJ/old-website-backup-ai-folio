Hakiim Jamaluddin is a Senior Lecturer in Statistics & Data Science at **Universiti Putra Malaysia**, Malaysia. He is a recent PhD graduate in Mathematics & Statistics from **UNSW Sydney**, Australia.

He completed his PhD training in the **UNSW Statistical Computing lab** at the UNSW Data Science Hub (fundamental Data Science team) ([uDASH](https://www.science.unsw.edu.au/engagement/data-science-hub)), advised by Professor [Scott Sisson](https://web.maths.unsw.edu.au/~scott/Welcome.html) and Dr. [Boris Beranger](https://www.borisberanger.com).

His research focuses on **Symbolic Data Analysis**, **Imbalanced Classification**, and **Computational Statistics**, bridging fundamental statistical methodologies with applied data science in healthcare and policy.

**Interests:** evening-runner, flat-white, table-tennis.
